    OUR PURPOSE

    OUR MISSION

    To empower women to make their own choices, run their own businesses, and live life on their own terms.

    Designed to Shine

    Crafted by hand. The old fashioned way.

    From start to finish. We design each and every piece that leaves our De'gold studio. Incorporating elements like vintage stones from the 1930's, re-purposing dead stock materials, we're on a mission to revive jewelry making in Chennai(once the leading industry in the state) in a sustainable and mindful way. Regarded by our collectors as "wearable art", it is our mission to create beautiful, heirloom quality costume jewelry that is cherished, and then passed down through generations.

    Designed to Shine

    We’re here to help women rise

    As moms, concerned citizens, and hard-working warriors for all women. Those facts are impossible to separate from the S&D business. And they also make us more than a business. We’re what change looks like and we’re on a mission. We’re grateful for your support and are here to make you proud.

    We give with every purchase

    Every time you shop,We round up to fund product donations to organizations that lift woman & children.Let's keep the momentum going.

    About us

    De'gold is a luxury designer jewelry house based in Chennai. We create seasonal collections of heirloom quality pieces that are crafted in small batches.
